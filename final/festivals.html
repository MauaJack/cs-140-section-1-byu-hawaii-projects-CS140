Major Festivals in PNG

A variety of festivals are celebrated throughout Papua New Guinea, showcasing rich traditions of music, dance, and art. Festivals like the Goroka Show and the Mount Hagen Cultural Show attract tourists and celebrate the diverse cultures across PNG.

The Goroka Show features tribes coming together to present their unique dance styles, colorful traditional attire, and music that tells stories of their lineage and beliefs.

Celebration Highlights

Music, dance, and storytelling are integral to festivals, serving as expressions of identity and community values. Attendees can learn about legends and history through performances that transcend generations.
