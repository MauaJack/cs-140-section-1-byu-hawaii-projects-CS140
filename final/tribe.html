Overview of Major Tribes

Papua New Guinea is home to many tribes, each with distinct traditions, languages, and customs. Significant tribes include the Huli, Sepik, and Tolai, each showcasing unique clothing, beliefs, and cultural practices.

The Huli people are best known for their elaborate wigs and colorful ceremonial attire, which they wear during festivals and tribal ceremonies.
