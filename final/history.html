Ancient Roots to Modern Nation

Papua New Guinea (PNG) has a rich and complex history. Human settlement dates back over 50,000 years. The island's tribes developed independently, leading to immense cultural and linguistic diversity.

During the colonial era, parts of PNG were controlled by Germany, Britain, and later Australia. PNG gained independence on September 16, 1975, becoming a sovereign nation with a parliamentary democracy.
