Village vs. City Life

Life in Papua New Guinea varies significantly between rural villages and urban centers. Many villagers still practice subsistence farming and maintain traditional ways of living, while cities are rapidly modernizing with growing economic opportunities.

Families in villages often live in kinship groups, sharing resources and responsibilities, while urban families experience fast-paced lifestyles, with jobs and schools shaping their daily routines.

Modern Changes in PNG

As Papua New Guinea navigates modernization, the influences from the global economy lead to both opportunities and challenges. New technologies are being introduced, reshaping communications, trade, and even cultural expressions.
