ENGAGEMENT QUESTIONS

  • What is the difference between block tags and inline tags?

    Block tags take up the full width of their container and start on a new line, while inline tags only take up as much width as necessary and do not start on a new line.

  • What is the margin, border, and padding?

    Margin is the space outside the element, border is the line surrounding the element, and padding is the space between the element's content and its border.

  • What are units of measurement you can use in CSS?

    In CSS, you can use units like px (pixels), em, rem, % (percentage), vw (viewport width), vh (viewport height), and cm (centimeters).

  • When would you use a figure tag?

    You would use a figure tag to group content such as an image and its caption, typically used for media or illustrations.
