My Biggest Influences in my life is my Father.

Father's Responsibility or Roles

Family Provider

Fathers work to ensure their children have their basic needs met, such as food, shelter, and clothing. They are family providers.

Family Protector

Fathers keep their children safe, both physically and emotionally, offering a sense of security. They are family protectors.

Role Model

Fathers set an example for their children by demonstrating values like integrity, responsibility, and respect. They are role models for their children.

Disciplining Children

Fathers enforce rules and teach their children the consequences of their actions, helping them understand boundaries. They discipline their children.

Teacher in the Home

Fathers impart life lessons and skills, guiding their children through both practical tasks and important decisions. They are also teachers in their home.
