Fathers Resposibility or Roles.

Father work to ensure their children have their basic needs met, such as food, shelter, and clothing.They are family Provider.

Father keep their children safe, both physically and emotionally, offering a sense of security. They are family Protector.

Father set an example for their children by demonstrating values like integrity, responsibility, and respect.They are their children Role Model

Father enforce rules and teach their children the consequences of their actions, helping them understand boundaries. They give Discipling their children.

Father impart life lessons and skills, guiding their children through both practical tasks and important decisions.They are also teacher in their home.
