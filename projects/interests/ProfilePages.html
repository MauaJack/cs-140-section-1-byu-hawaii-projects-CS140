Interest

I am originally from Papua New Guinea, Born and raised in the National Capital District, Port Moresby.
I come from a mixed heritage, with roots in both Morobe ad Gulf Province in Papua New Guinea.I am 27 years Old. In terms my education, I attended elementary and primary school in kilakila, then continue my high school studies at kilakila high school from 2014-2015.
I had the opportunity to study at Liahona High School in Tonga 2016-2018.In 2019, I was called to serve a mission in Ghana, West Africa, in the Cape Coast Mission.I served there until 2021, during the COVID-19 pandemic, and was reassigned to three different missions: Brisbane, Port Moresby, and Lae.
On December 21, 2022, I was admitted to Brigham Young University–Hawaii (BYUH) to pursue a bachelor’s degree in Information Technology. I am currently a sophomore student.
